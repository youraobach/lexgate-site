How it works

A light gate, not a wall.

LexGate uses Apple's Screen Time technology to stand between you and the apps that eat your day — gently.

Choose your apps

Pick the apps you want to limit using Apple's standard selection screen. One or several — your call.

The gate appears

When you tap a blocked app, LexGate steps in with a full-screen exercise instead of the feed.

Translate one word

Pick the correct translation from four options. Words match your level and return on a spaced-repetition schedule.

Unlocked — for a while

A correct answer opens the app for 5 minutes. When time runs out, the gate quietly returns.

Privacy

Everything stays on your device.

Fully offline

The word bank, your progress, and all blocking logic live on your iPhone. LexGate works without an internet connection.

No accounts, no tracking

No sign-up, no analytics SDKs, no ads. We never see which apps you block or which words you learn.

Apple's own controls

Blocking is powered by Apple's Screen Time technology, authorized by you, for you. You can revoke access at any time in Settings.
